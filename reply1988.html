  • Reply 1988







    • Main Cast:

      • Lee Hye-Ri as Sung Deok-Sun, Go Kyung-Pyo as Sung Sun-Woo, Ryu Joon-Yeol as Kim Jang-Hwan, Park Bo-Gum as Choi Taek, Lee Dong-Hwi as Ryu Dong-Ryong

    • Writing Credits:

      • Lee Woo-Jung

    • Genres:

      • Comedy, Romance, Life, Youth

    • Runtime:

      • 1 hour and 35 minutes







    Five childhood friends, who all live in the same Ssangmundong neighborhood of Seoul, lean on each other to survive their challenging teen years and set a path for their futures.


    “The year was 1988, a time when it was chilly, but our hearts were fiery, a time when we didn’t have much but people’s hearts were warm.” - Sung Deok-Sun





    There is rarely a drama like Reply 1988 that comes out once in a while and totally steals your heart. Never have I seen a drama so well-written, acted and executed so well that it sucks me in and makes me smile and cry as each moment passes by. The family of the drama is one whole consisting of so many unique people. These people define a family- being together through the good times and bad and cherishing each other no matter what. I happen to be a person glad to be living in the 21st century but this drama made me wanna go back to the 80's.


    I fell in love with every single family. I shared the parents’ worries and fears, I had fun along with the five friends of Ssangmun-dong and, at the end, I felt like I grew up with them because they taught me so many life lessons.
