    Modern Family centers on the daily lives of three different but related families. There's Jay and his second, much younger wife, Gloria, and her son, Manny. Jay's grown daughter, Claire, lives nearby with her husband, Phil, and is a full-time mom to their three teens. Claire's brother, Mitchell, and his life partner, Cameron, are raising their adopted daughter a stone's throw away from the rest of the family. The series chronicles the ups and downs of parenting, marriage, and family relationships.


    “When life gives you lemonade, make lemons. Life will be all like, ‘What?!’” – Phil Dunphy
